Privacy Policy for Mythic Idle

Last updated: 27 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the mobile game Mythic Idle ("the Game", "we", "us") handles information when you play it. The Game is developed and operated by an independent developer. By installing or playing the Game, you agree to the practices described below.

The Game does not require you to create an account, and it does not ask for your name, email address, phone number, or any other directly identifying personal information.

1. Information We Collect

a) Game progress (stored on your device)
Your save data — including your level, resources, gear, and overall progress — is stored locally on your device. It is not uploaded to us, except for the limited leaderboard data described below.

b) Leaderboard data
The Game includes an optional global leaderboard. If you choose a display name, the following information is sent to and stored on our leaderboard server (Google Firebase Realtime Database):

  • A display name that you choose (please do not enter real personal information as your name).
  • Game progress figures such as experience (XP) and prestige/ascension level.
  • A randomly generated anonymous identifier created on your device. This identifier is not linked to your real-world identity.

This leaderboard information is publicly visible to other players inside the Game.

c) Advertising data
The Game shows optional rewarded video advertisements provided by Google AdMob. When ads are served, Google may collect and process information such as your device's advertising identifier, IP address, device type and operating system, and general interaction data, in order to deliver and measure advertisements. We do not control and do not receive this data directly.

d) Analytics data
The Game uses Google Firebase Analytics to understand how players use the Game so we can improve it. This may include device model, operating system version, app version, in-game events (such as completing a level), approximate region derived from your IP address, and an app-instance identifier. This data is aggregated and is not used to identify you personally.

e) Notifications
The Game may request permission to send local notifications (for example, reminders that an in-game timer is ready). These notifications are generated on your device. No personal data is collected through this feature, and you can disable notifications at any time in your device settings.

2. How We Use Information

  • To save and restore your game progress on your device.
  • To operate the optional global leaderboard.
  • To display advertisements that help support the Game.
  • To analyse usage trends and improve gameplay, balance, and stability.
  • To send optional gameplay reminders via local notifications.

3. Third-Party Services

The Game relies on the following third-party services, each of which has its own privacy policy:

  • Google AdMob — advertising. Google Privacy Policy · AdMob & Privacy
  • Google Firebase (Analytics & Realtime Database) — analytics and leaderboard storage. Firebase Privacy
  • Google Play — app distribution. Google Privacy Policy

4. Advertising Choices & Consent

Where required by law (for example, for players in the European Economic Area and the United Kingdom), the Game will ask for your consent before serving personalised advertisements, using Google's consent management tools. You can also reset or limit ad personalisation at any time through your device's settings (for example, "Ads" or "Privacy" settings on Android, where you can reset your advertising ID or opt out of ad personalisation).

5. Data Retention & Deletion

Local game data remains on your device until you delete it or uninstall the Game. Leaderboard entries remain on our server until they are overwritten by newer progress or removed. If you would like your leaderboard entry deleted, please contact us at the email address below and include the display name you used, and we will remove the corresponding entry.

6. Children's Privacy

The Game is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

7. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the limited data the Game handles. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the Game after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
